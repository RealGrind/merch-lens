# Merch Lens

Merch Lens is a RuneLite external plugin for Grand Exchange market watching and flip research.

It shows OSRS Wiki market data, post-tax margins, buy limits, volume, freshness, 24-hour trend estimates, favorites, item lookup, active GE offer tracking, a local realized Flip Log, and a profitable-only market screener inside RuneLite.

Merch Lens is a beta research tool. Verify trades manually before buying or selling.

## Features

- Compact profit-calculator utility action, plus icon navigation for favorites, high-volume items, the Screener, and Flip Log.
- Toggleable market-control panels to leave more room for item cards in small client windows.
- Search any OSRS item by name or item ID.
- Optional inventory, bank, equipment, and ground-item right-click `Merch` lookup that opens the sidebar to that item.
- Post-tax margin, full buy-limit profit, and observed-flow profit estimates.
- Buy volume/hr, sell volume/hr, and buy/sell ratio.
- Market Screener sorting by flow profit, limit profit, ROI, buy price, net margin, or hourly volume.
- Price freshness and 24-hour trend labels.
- Daily and weekly price chart popouts from OSRS Wiki timeseries data.
- Display-only GE offer overlay with minimap-safe compact mode, exact hover details, and local timers.
- Local Flip Log with timeframe, item search, profit sorting, pagination, and confirmed timeframe resets.
- Local-only active offer state, flip cost basis/results, and favorites stored in RuneLite profile config.

## Data Source

Merch Lens fetches read-only market data from the OSRS Wiki Prices API. It does not place, cancel, collect, or automate Grand Exchange offers.
